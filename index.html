Welcome to Barry Blue Books

Barry Blue Books, founded in 2005, is an independent lending library situated in the centre of Riga. It houses nearly 20,000 books and has a tranquil and relaxed atmosphere. In addition to the several cozy reading rooms, there is a beautiful garden, which is a wonderful hideout in the warm season.

The library is a great place to relax, read a book and meet friends over a cup of coffee or speciality tea. We welcome new members and there are plenty of opportunities to visit us.
